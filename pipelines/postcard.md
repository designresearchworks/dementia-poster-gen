## Interpretation Prompt

Consider these ideas {extracted-concepts}. I want to imagine a world where these ideas all co-exist, and somebody is sending a postcard home. Perhaps they are talking about a technology, or innovation that somehow relates to these ideas. What would be on this postcard, and what's the context be? It could be a person living with dementia writing home, or their family writing to them, or a friend writing. Why did these words end up on a postcard? Think that through, then describe the postcard.
