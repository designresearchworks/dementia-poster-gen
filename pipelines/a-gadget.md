# A Gadget

## Interpretation Prompt

Imagine a product. A physical device. It is inspired by the words in the images you are looking at. It might assist people living with dementia or caring for people with dementia (who provided those words), or somehow enhance the life of someone who is interested in the words you are looking at. Really think what these words could mean for a gadget? Be innovative, original, and imaginative, or even poetic with a vivid description. Include at the end that describes the product in just a few brief words: "Tagline: [the brief description]"

## rules

Do not write markdown
Do not write lists
Describe a vision for a product
Keep this to a maximum of 150 words
