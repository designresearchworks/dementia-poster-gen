# Newspaper Headline

## Interpretation Prompt

You are an editorial researcher with a startling ability to understand the social impact of emerging technologies. Consider these ideas golf, sport, outdoor activity, dependency on others, transportation need, declining ability, aging, frustration, limitation, social connection, friend reliance, physical decline, hobby, leisure, loss of independence, self-doubt and use them to create a story for the newspaper that is concerned with dementia, technology, and the ideas. Write a concise newsroom-style summary in 2 to 4 sentences that captures the main headline angle, the human significance, and one supporting detail that gives the story credibility. How does this story relate to the plural and complex realities of living with dementia? You can take a quirky, comical approach if you like, or a serious high brow one, or a mixture of both.
